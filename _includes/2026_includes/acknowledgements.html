Acknowledgements

UC Love Data Week is made possible by the collaborative effort of librarians and staff across the University of California system.

We would like to thank the campus contacts for each year, for their work on developing presentations, wrangling presenters, ensuring consistent communication, and otherwise making this event a truly cross-campus experience.

We would also like to extend our gratitude to the various library communication and events folks who worked tirelessly to set up and handle registration and recordings of these events.
